Over my life, I have cultivated a love of learning. As a professional scholar of History, I traveled to far-flung lands seeking out forgotten voices and conveyed their wisdom to hundreds of students. Today, as a web developer specializing in educational technologies, I employ the tools of the Digital Age to enable others to reach their potential and discover new worlds. I am a lifelong learner in a profession where constant change demands continual learning, adaptation, and growth.
